Final project

This project utilizes the CAMELS (Catchment Attributes and MEteorology for Large-sample Studies)

dataset available through NCAR Research Applications Laboratory.

Precipitation (mm/day) and runoff (mm/day) data for 15 USGS stream gauges were used for the plots.



Mean annual precipitation is averaged over the entire 12 month period for any given year.

Available data for gauge 08050800 begins on October 1, 1985.


Total monthly precipitation is summed over each month for all years of record.

Available data for gauge 08050800 begins on October 1, 1985.


Mean monthly precipitation is averaged over each month for all years of record.

Available data for gauge 08050800 begins on October 1, 1985.


Runoff Efficiency

Runoff efficiency is runoff/precipitation. Annual means were used.


Runoff efficiency is based on overall annual means.
